## 4. Verification rules

### What must be confirmed before publishing
Business name  
Deal title  
Day  
Time  
Neighborhood  
Valid address  
Phone if available  
Link, menu, post, or other proof if available

### What counts as enough proof
An official website page  
An official Instagram or Facebook post from the business  
A current menu or flyer from the business  
A direct submission through the form with enough detail to verify  
A recent direct confirmation from the business

### What requires follow up
Missing day or time  
Conflicting time ranges across sources  
No source link or proof  
Unclear address or wrong neighborhood  
No way to confirm the deal is current  
One time or seasonal deal without dates  
Submission that sounds promotional but not specific enough to publish

## 5. Google Form submission workflow

### Where submissions are reviewed
Google Form responses  
Linked Google Sheet for form entries  
Then copied into the weekly working list if approved or pending verification

### Approval process
1. Check the form for complete contact info, day, time, and deal details  
2. Verify the deal through the source link, business page, or direct follow up  
3. Add it to the site only after the details are clear enough to publish confidently

### Rejection process
1. Mark the submission as rejected if the details are incomplete, unverifiable, outdated, or too vague  
2. If the business seems legitimate but the info is weak, request clarification before rejecting  
3. Do not publish anything that could mislead users about the actual deal

### Follow up needed when
The hours are missing  
The day is unclear  
The deal description is vague  
The source link does not confirm the deal  
The address or neighborhood is wrong  
The business submitted a one time or seasonal deal without dates

## 6. Updating live deals

### How deals get added to the site
1. Confirm the deal details and source  
2. Add or update the structured deal data in the project content files  
3. Regenerate or export the static pages, then verify the pages before publishing

### How expired or wrong deals get removed
1. Remove or correct the deal in the source content as soon as it is confirmed wrong or expired  
2. Re export the affected pages  
3. Check the homepage, Today page, and affected day or neighborhood page after the update
