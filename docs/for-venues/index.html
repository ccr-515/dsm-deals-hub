About DSM Deals Hub

Why I built DSM Deals Hub

I built DSM Deals Hub because too many good deals are scattered all over the place. Some are buried in Instagram stories, some are on Facebook, some are outdated, and some are impossible to find unless you already know where to look. I wanted one clean place where people in Des Moines could quickly see what is worth going out for today, what is happening right now, and what each neighborhood has to offer. At the same time, I wanted it to help local businesses get more attention on the deals they actually need people to see.

DSM Deals Hub is curated manually from local posts and business updates.
